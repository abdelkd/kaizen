# Percentage - Finding Percentage by Number


## Definition

To find a certain oercentage out of a number we turn the percentage into a decimal then multiply decimal by the number.

**Examples:** what is 34% of 44?
- 34 / 100 = 0.34
- 0.34 * 44 = 14.96%
